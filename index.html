Main Features

Cubie provides personalized homepages, smart dashboards, and AI-powered practice tools to support students, teachers, and families. The platform makes it easy for teachers to manage lessons, upload homework, and give feedback, helping everyone stay connected and focused on learning.

Role-Based Homepage

Each user — student, teacher and family — gets a personalized homepage designed for their unique needs.

Content Management

Teachers can easily manage lesson materials, upload homework, and give feedback on student submissions using a clear and organized dashboard.

AI-Powered Smart Practice

Integrated AI creates personalized practice sessions by generating age-appropriate questions from lesson content.

Smart Dashboard & Data Visualization

A core feature that delivers clear, visual insights into student learning, tailored for both teachers and families.
